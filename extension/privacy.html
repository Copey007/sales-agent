Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

A-Gent is an AI-powered productivity tracking tool for sales professionals. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Bottom line: We don't track your browsing history. Your data stays on your device. We only see aggregate time categories — not the specific pages you visit.

What We Collect

A-Gent tracks aggregate category data only — not individual URLs or page content. Specifically, we collect:

  • Time spent in category buckets: Email, CRM, Chat, Docs, Social, Web, Other
  • Daily totals and session counts
  • Your email address (for identification across devices, if you choose to provide it)

How We Store Data

All tracking data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not have access to this data. We do not operate any servers that receive your browsing data.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't track individual URLs or page content
  • We don't sell or share your data
  • We don't show ads
  • We don't integrate with third-party analytics

Data Retention

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the extension or using the "Stop Tracking" button in the app. There is no retention period because we simply don't hold your data.

Children

A-Gent is not intended for users under 16 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
